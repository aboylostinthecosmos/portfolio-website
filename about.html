About Me

a boy lost in the cosmos

I’m Kumar Shivam, a science student driven by curiosity and wonder. Since childhood, I’ve been fascinated by the cosmos — the mysteries of the universe and the beauty of physics that explains them.

My Journey

When I was in class 6th, I used to watch TV shows like Ancient Aliens and How the Universe Was Made on History TV 18 and Discovery Science. Those shows sparked a deep curiosity within me — a desire to understand this vast cosmos and uncover its hidden mysteries. Life brought its share of challenges, but that curiosity never faded. Today, I’m continuing that same dream — to study and explore the universe scientifically.
